The Avengers

Iron Man

Tony Stark is the genius billionaire behind the Iron Man suit. He is the founding member of the Avengers. He uses his advanced technology to save the world.

Captain America

Steve Rogers is a super-soldier from World War II. He is a symbol of hope and justice. He wields an unbreakable shield.

Thor

Thor Odinson is the Norse god of thunder. He protects Earth and his home world Asgard. He uses his powerful hammer, Mjölnir.

Hulk

Bruce Banner is a brilliant scientist who turns into the massive green beast known as the Hulk when he gets angry. He is the strongest hero there is.
